# REMi demo flow

## Primary sequence

1. Open REMi on the home page.
2. Notice the breathing moon, soft signal waves, and synthetic-data label.
3. Select **Run tonight's plan**.
4. Watch the orchestration stages complete in sequence:
   - User Signals
   - Data Pipeline
   - Risk Engine
   - Nebius Plan
   - Tavily Evidence
   - Composio Actions
   - ElevenLabs Voice
   - Tonight Plan
5. Review the high-friction wellness estimate and its transparent drivers.
6. Reveal the **Luteal Downshift** protocol.
7. Review the mock evidence context.
8. Show the mocked calendar, sleep log, reminder, and save actions.
9. Select the voice preview control to animate the demo waveform.
10. Use the bottom navigation to show the focused Tonight, Protocol, and
    Actions views.

## Demo narrative

REMi does not stop at a score. It translates signals into a calm explanation,
chooses a bounded wellness routine, grounds the routine with context, prepares
real-world actions, and ends with a warm voice briefing.

The supplied synthetic night has a risk score of `10`, a `high` risk level, and
selects the **Luteal Downshift** protocol starting at `9:40 PM`.
